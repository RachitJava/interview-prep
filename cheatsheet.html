⚡ Quick Interview One-Liners

"Tell me about X"Your Answer
"What is RAG?"Instead of making the LLM memorize everything, we retrieve relevant docs from a vector DB and feed them as context — so it always has up-to-date, accurate info.
"What is LoRA?"It freezes 99% of model weights and only trains tiny adapter layers — fine-tune a 7B model on a single GPU in hours instead of weeks.
"What is Knowledge Distillation?"Use a big expensive model (GPT-4) as teacher to generate training data, then train a small free model (Llama) to produce the same quality output.
"Why microservices?"Independent deployment, scaling, and team ownership. At Sabre, we went from 1 big deploy to 8 services — deploy time dropped 60%.
"What is CAP Theorem?"In a distributed system, you can only guarantee 2 of 3: Consistency, Availability, Partition Tolerance. Most systems pick AP + eventual consistency.
"Why Docker + K8s?"Docker packages the app identically everywhere. K8s auto-scales — traffic spikes, it spins up more containers automatically.
"What is a Vector DB?"Stores AI embeddings — numerical representations of text — and finds semantically similar items. We use Pinecone in our RAG pipeline.
"What is event-driven arch?"Services publish events to Kafka instead of calling each other. Loose coupling, better scalability, independent failure handling.
